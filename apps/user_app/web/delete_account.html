Delete Account & Data – Skilnk App

If you are a user of the "Skilnk" app and wish to delete your account and personal data:

Steps to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open the Skilnk app
  2. Go to: Profile > Settings > Delete Account
  3. Confirm your choice

Upon deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your uploaded content or profile data
  • Chat history and enrolled courses

No personal data is retained after deletion. We retain payment records only as required by law.
